INTRODUCTION
In the vast expanse of the universe, on a remote and uncharted
alien world known as Idzara, life had thrived for eons,
blissfully unaware of the existence of other sentient beings
beyond their celestial borders. The Idzarans, curious and
resilient, had woven the fabric of their society from the very
essence of their unique planet, nurturing a world where
harmony prevailed, and the pursuit of knowledge was revered
above all else.
For countless years, they had gazed upon the heavens,
wondering if they were truly alone in the cosmos. It was a
belief that had become an article of faith among the Idzarans,
an unspoken pact with the universe itself. But little did they
know that their celestial solitude was about to be shattered, and
their world would bear witness to an invasion like no other.
As the foreboding shadow of alien invaders loomed above, their
tranquil existence stood on the precipice of upheaval and
transformation. The tale of "Invaders From Above" would
unfold in three distinct character arcs, each narrating a unique
perspective in this epic saga.

Chapter 1: "Slum Reality"
In the heart of a sprawling megacity, hidden amidst the

souls eke out their existence.
Our protagonist, a techno-punk living on the fringes of this
desolation, navigates the daily struggles of a life confined to
