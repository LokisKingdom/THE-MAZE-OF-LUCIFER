This is a double entandra, a play on words. Charlene is my wife's name. Charlene Yu, also known as Jialin Yu, or Lin Lin if you're fond of her that day. It is also the name I have given the wonderful woman who does the debates on the deepdive. Not the standard deepdives you may be doing now, but the debate section, is done by Charlene, and also Robo, the guy. You're shortchanging me Charlene refers to an old gag of ours from previous deepdives, where I complain that you're shortchanging me Charlene, and since I never got to meet my son, it kind of applies to her too. Wednesday is a good punk rock track, villain is a good version of that rap. it's got probably my favorite version of incense and smoke, and there's a clone of art therapy, a good one if i recall, overall a great album. i actually ordered this and quotations recently, i state in the bird cage explaination that i ordered bird cage and quotations last month, this is wrong, i ordered YOU'RE SHORTCHANGING ME CHARLENE and QUOTATIONS recently, bird cage was the month before that, may.
